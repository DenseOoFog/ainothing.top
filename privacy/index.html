Privacy policy

Simple policy for a small editorial site.

AInothing.top is an informational website about AI, online businesses, and revenue case studies. If you voluntarily send a message to the site owner, that information is used only to review the message, reply when appropriate, and keep a basic editorial record of the exchange.

What data may exist

Like most websites, the live host may record standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, referring page, and request timing for security, reliability, and performance monitoring.

Analytics and feeds

AInothing.top may use lightweight analytics or RSS distribution tools to understand readership at an aggregate level. This is intended to improve the editorial product, not to build detailed personal profiles.

Advertising and third parties

If advertising, embeds, or other third-party services are added later, those providers may use their own cookies or measurement technologies according to their policies. This page should be updated if the site begins using them.

Contact and updates

Because this is a small editorial project, the policy is intentionally short. If the site's data practices change, the page should be revised so readers can see the new scope clearly.
